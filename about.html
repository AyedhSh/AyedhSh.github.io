Hello!
A bit about me:

My name is Ayedh and I grew up east of Anbar, in Fallujah and I was displaced to Erbil in 2014. After high school, I studied computer science and information technology, then worked in Iraqi Ministry of Education for several years. While I was studying and working I found that I was always interested in coding and tried to learn it in my spare time. After 1 year from displacement, I decided it was time to change my life and improve my programing skills, so I signed up to go to the Re:Coded Program. It was the best program to help me achieve my goals. I am interested to work as a developer, and to evolve and expand my skills. Another goal is to teach other people coding. Learning programming is still lots of fun and one of the best decisions I've ever made.
